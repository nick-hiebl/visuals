The three respective panels show:

  • The best route found so far via a genetic algorithm search.
  • A random variation considered in the genetic algorithm.
  • A 2-approximation produced via a MST method. The green lines represent some edges of the MST, however many are covered by edges used in the route.

The fact that the third panel shows a 2-approximation is quite significant. Its score must be within a factor or 2 of the optimal Traveling Salesperson route, so it can be used to approximate the effectiveness of the route found by the genetic search.
